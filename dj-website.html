About Me

Hey guys! My name is Stephen Ray Guedea, aka DJ Stevie G, I'm born and raised in San Antonio, TX, home of the San Antonio Spurs! I've been a DJ since 1999, started helping my dad set up at event, then actually started playing behind the tables at my grandma's backyard parties. One thing lead to another and next thing you know, a hobby become a career! I've played and hosted numerous events, both private and public, and I'm determined, no matter how big or small the crowd is, to make sure that everyone will have a great time. It's your party, I'm just the DJ. My music library consists of, but not limited to, the following:

  • Top 40
  • Hip-Hop and R&B
  • Cumbias
  • Tejano
  • Reggaeton
  • Disco
  • New Wave
  • Any other genre can be requested
